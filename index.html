How it works

Behavioral AI · Social Engineering · Enterprise SaaS

Traditional security training assumes every employee is the same risk. Veros doesn't. The platform builds a behavioral profile for each user — tracking how they process, respond, and rationalize decisions under pressure — then uses that profile to deploy social engineering simulations specifically calibrated to their patterns.

Veros encompasses both functions in one platform: behavioral profiling and the attack engine. Powered by the Aether Behavioral Framework — it governs how data flows, how profiles are structured, and how per-user encryption keys are derived (HKDF) — keeping profile data isolated and non-transferable. That's novel, defensible IP.
